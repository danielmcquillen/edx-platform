Jessica Tashker serves as Director in the Market Analysis and Strategy group at Genentech. Jessica has 10+ years experience in the biotech and pharmaceutical industry. Currently in her role, she helps the company to make informed, data-driven decisions on market strategy. Prior to Genentech she worked at the Institute for OneWorld Health, a non-profit pharmaceutical company, focusing on early stage drug development for childhood diarrheal disease. She holds a Ph.D. in Molecular Cancer Biology from Duke University.

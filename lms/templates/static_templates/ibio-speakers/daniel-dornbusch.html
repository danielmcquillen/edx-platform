Daniel Dornbusch has served in multiple management roles at pharmaceutical, biotechnology, diagnostics, and strategy consulting companies including Novartis, Genentech, Amerisource Bergen and Genzyme. Daniel has spent almost 20 years in the healthcare industry in various roles including business development, sales, marketing, corporate development, and commercial management. Daniel is a founder of KidsDOC, a telemedicine company, as well as Acteris, a cancer immunotherapy drug development company where he served as CEO. Daniel received his B.A. in Biology and English from Cornell, M.A. in Science Communication from Tufts University School of Medicine and Emerson College, and M.B.A. from the Harvard Business School.
